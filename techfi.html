EDC TechFi is the subchapter of BVP EDC with Mr. Vaibhav Sharma as its Chairperson and Mr. Arvind Sharma as Vice-Chairperson. With a concept of "Grow Money from Money.", EDC Techfi aims to provide financial literacy to students.
VISION
The vision of Edc Techfi is to empower students with the knowledge and skills needed to make informed financial decisions throughout their lives. We strive to create a community where financial literacy is prioritized and accessible to all, regardless of background or socioeconomic status. Our goal is to inspire students to take control of their finances, build wealth, and achieve their financial goals. We believe that by educating the next generation of leaders in financial literacy, we can create a more financially savvy and prosperous society.
MISSION
EDC Techfi with the mission - "Grow money from money" aims to provide skills to help students make the right decisions regarding their personal finances, including budgeting, saving, investing, and debt management. Through our workshops, seminars, and resources, we strive to equip students with the necessary tools to achieve financial stability and success both during and after their college years.
